Control Panels

Front panels, overlays, and legend plates that keep complex equipment clearly labeled and operator-ready.

Draft specifications for TNC review. Materials and capabilities below are best-faith drafts. Values marked confirm — thicknesses, sizes, finishes, adhesives, and lead times — need TNC sign-off, and product photography is pending.

Overview

Control panels, front panels, and graphic overlays put information exactly where an operator needs it. We build these from anodized aluminum, stainless, or printed polycarbonate, with cutouts, windows, and embossing to fit the hardware behind them.

Most panels combine several of our processes — screen or digital printing for graphics, plus punching and forming for the mechanical features.
